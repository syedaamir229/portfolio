## Results from Real Work

<div class="grid cards what-changed" markdown>

-   :material-database:{ .lg .middle } **Data Foundation**

    ---

    **Before:** Four source systems, no shared model, every team using their own query logic.

    **After:** One adopted data model that resolved metric disputes and became the base for semantic layers, ML features, and AI automation.

    [Full case study](projects/data-model.md)

-   :material-robot:{ .lg .middle } **CRM Automation**

    ---

    **Before:** Every campaign audience was built by request through the data team, creating a constant handoff bottleneck.

    **After:** CRM teams configure and launch campaigns independently through a governed scenario engine connected to CleverTap.

    [Full case study](projects/jarvis.md)

-   :material-chat-processing:{ .lg .middle } **Social Intelligence**

    ---

    **Before:** Arabic social feedback at scale with no practical way to analyze it consistently or at speed.

    **After:** A multilingual intelligence platform with NLP, Genie-based querying, and a conversational interface non-technical teams can use directly.

    [Full case study](projects/enigma.md)

## Why Work With Me

<div class="grid cards what-changed" markdown>

-   :material-database:{ .lg .middle } **Data Architecture and Engineering**

    ---

    At some point, every growing business ends up with the same number meaning different things depending on where you look. A shared data model fixes that. I built one at scale for Shahid (MBC Group) with millions of subscribers, and it became the foundation for every analytics, ML, and AI project that followed.

    [See the project](projects/data-model.md)

-   :material-chart-bar:{ .lg .middle } **BI and Analytics**

    ---

    When your key metrics are defined inside individual reports, they drift. One place where numbers are calculated consistently, across every tool and every team, is what makes reporting trustworthy. It is also what makes a BI platform migration survivable.

    [Read the case study](projects/semantic-layer.md)

-   :material-brain:{ .lg .middle } **Data Science and ML**

    ---

    Behavioral data only pays off when it feeds into decisions. I build segmentation and inference workflows that connect what users actually do with what planning and marketing teams need to act on.

    ---

    Manual data requests, repetitive reporting, and audience-building by hand add up to hours that should not exist. I build automation systems that remove those bottlenecks, from campaign creation to natural-language querying over your own business data.
