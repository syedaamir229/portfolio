# Financial & Sales Reporting Platform

!!! abstract "Case Study Summary"
    **Organization**: Adani Enterprises (via Scan Technology LLC)
    **Role**: BI Consultant
    **Timeline**: May 2017 - December 2017
    **Industry**: Conglomerate / Financial Services
    **Ownership**: Primary implementer for data workflow automation and dashboard delivery

    **Impact Metrics**:

    - Improved report accessibility for finance and sales stakeholders
    - Reduced recurring report preparation effort through automation
    - Replaced manual spreadsheet consolidation with standardized reporting flows
    - **30% improvement in data flow efficiency** through Alteryx-based data modeling and integration
    - **25% faster report generation** through optimized SQL queries and data models

Finance and sales reporting depended on manual spreadsheet operations, which slowed cycles and reduced confidence in recurring outputs.

## Challenge

- **Manual reporting load**: Teams spent significant time preparing recurring reports
- **Data preparation inconsistency**: Multiple source extracts required repeated cleanup steps
- **Limited scalability**: Spreadsheet-based workflows did not scale with reporting demand

## Approach

- Built Alteryx workflows for recurring extraction, transformation, and validation
- Designed Tableau dashboards for financial and sales performance monitoring
- Standardized report logic and delivery cadence for business consumers
- Reduced manual handoffs by automating routine preparation steps

## Results & Impact

- Reporting cycles became more predictable and easier to maintain
- Finance and sales teams accessed clearer recurring views with less delay
- Manual consolidation effort dropped as automated workflows matured

## Tech Stack

- Alteryx
- Tableau
- SQL
- Excel

## Reusable Pattern

This manual-to-automated reporting transition applies across many sectors:

- **Enterprise finance**: Recurring management and variance reporting
- **Sales operations**: Pipeline and performance reporting automation
- **Shared services**: Standardized reporting for cross-functional teams

## Additional Context

- Delivered as part of early consulting engagements focused on practical BI modernization
