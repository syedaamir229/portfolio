## Featured Projects

<div class="grid cards" markdown>

-   [Enterprise Data Model](data-model.md)

    ---

    **Fragmented source systems → unified Silver/Gold model trusted across reporting, ML, and automation.**

    **5 source systems unified · 30% faster retrieval · 99% data accuracy**

    Unified disconnected source systems into a governed data model used across reporting, ML, and automation workflows. **Databricks, PySpark, SQL**

    <span class="project-tag">Data Engineering</span>

-   [Semantic Layer & KPI Framework](semantic-layer.md)

    ---

    **Disputed metrics across teams → single governed KPI layer with consistent definitions.**

    **100+ governed DAX measures · 50% faster queries · 15 hrs/week saved**

    Standardized KPI logic in one semantic layer to eliminate metric drift and cut report build time. **SSAS, DAX, Power BI**

    <span class="project-tag">BI & Analytics</span>

-   [Revenue Operations Pipeline & Alerting Platform](ad-pipeline.md)

    ---

    **Spreadsheet-tracked revenue ops → real-time pipeline with proactive pacing alerts.**

    **4 production pipelines · Same-day Slack alerts · 3 teams unified**

    Connected ad requests, impressions, pacing, and errors into a monitored event-to-revenue pipeline. **Databricks, Python, Slack, GAM API**

    <span class="project-tag">Data Engineering</span>

-   [BI Modernization Roadmap](bi-migration.md)

    ---

    **Fragmented legacy BI → governed semantic-layer reporting across the organization.**

    **3 phases, zero disruption · 35% lower maintenance · 40% higher adoption**

    Led a phased modernization from legacy tooling to Power BI, then to shared data and semantic-layer reporting. **Power BI, Tableau, Databricks**

    <span class="project-tag">BI & Analytics</span>

-   [CRM Campaign Automation Platform](jarvis.md)

    ---

    **Manual campaign creation → automated scenario engine with feedback loops.**

    **Millions of profiles/day · 4 recommendation scenarios · Multi-day → daily automation**

    Automated scenario creation, scheduled activation, and campaign performance feedback for CRM operations. **Python, CleverTap API, Databricks**

    <span class="project-tag">AI & Automation</span>
    <span class="project-tag">Data Engineering</span>

-   [Voice-of-Customer Intelligence Platform](enigma.md)

    ---

    **Unstructured multilingual feedback → searchable insight layer with natural language Q&A.**

    **4 social sources unified · Hours → seconds via NL query · 2 Genie spaces + Supervisor Agent**

    Structured social and product feedback, then enabled NL query access through Genie and retrieval workflows. **Databricks, NLP, RAG, Genie**

    <span class="project-tag">AI & Automation</span>
    <span class="project-tag">Data Science</span>
